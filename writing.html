Book

I have also writen a novel: Thalci Varner and the Red Children of Paradise. It is hard sci-fi, set in a future where humanity has sent out a generation ship to colonize another planet. The ship, however, has taken so long to arrive on the new planet that it's inhabitents have forgotten where they have come from. The story begins when they finally land on this new planet, and our characters must struggle to overcome their personal and cultural crises as they reconcile their own beliefs with the bizarre new world they discover around themselves.

I had done quite a bit of wiring on my own time before this (also in school, of course), but this was by far the largest project I have ever completed. I finished the first draft in about six months, by the end of December 2017, and published it the following Febuary. I am quite pleased with the result, and I have recieved good feedback from my family and friends who have read it. If you are interested, it is avalible through Amazon, in both print and Kindle formats, and also on Kobo in ebook format.

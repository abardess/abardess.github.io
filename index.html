Welcome!

My name is Anthony Bardessono.

I am a graduate of California Polytechnic State University, where I received a bachelors in physics and a minor in mathematics. I have over three years experience coding in Python, as well as experience using LaTeX, Mathematica, and MatLab. I have worked on several mathematical modeling projects during my time at Cal Poly and have experience with a variety of laboratory instrumentation.

Most recently I worked as an instructor at AstroCamp, VA, where I helped teach students about a variety of science topics. In addition to managing students, I helped develop many programs for the burgeoning camp and created a variety of documentation including instruction manuals, learning aids, and lesson plans. I also gained experince in building and maintaining a variety of circuitry, including a set of ANET A8 3D printers. Currently I am working toward a teaching credential while looking for work in California, ideally near the Bay Area.

I also do a lot of reading and writing. I have published a science fiction novel through Amazon (sequel in the works). And I have a blog where I post my thoughts and recommendations about the books I read.

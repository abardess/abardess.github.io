About Writing Academics

Welcome!

My name is Anthony Bardessono. I am a graduate of California Polytechnic State University, where I received a bachelors in physics and a minor in mathematics. I have over three years experience coding in Python, as well as experience using LaTeX, Mathematica, and MatLab. I have worked on several mathematical modeling projects during my time at Cal Poly and have experience with a variety of laboratory instrumentation. I am currently working as an instructor at AstroCamp Virginia, helping teaching science to visiting students and enriching their education.

I also do a lot of reading and writing. I have published a science fiction novel through Amazon, and I have recently started a blog where I post my thoughts and recommendations about the books I read.

I can be reached via Email, or through any social media you find me on.

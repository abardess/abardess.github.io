Welcome!

My name is Anthony Bardessono. I am a graduate of both California Polytechnic State University and Sonoma State University. At Cal Poly I received a bachelors in physics and a minor in mathematics, and at SSU I recieved a credential for teaching Secondary Science in Physics and Chemistry. Before achieving my credential, I had worked in educational settings with children for over five years. I also have experience usign coding languages such as LaTeX, Mathematica, MatLab, and the Godot Engine. I worked on several mathematical modeling projects during my time at Cal Poly and have experience with a variety of laboratory instrumentation. I am also a self-published author.

I have tutored mathematics and physics on and off since 2012, teaching middle-, high-school, and freshman college students in one-on-one sessions. I tutored independantly, as well as through SurferMath and Sylvan Tutoring.

After my undergraduate studies, I worked as an instructor at AstroCamp, VA, where I helped teach students about a variety of science topics. In addition to managing small classess, I helped develop many programs for the burgeoning camp and created a variety of documentation including instruction manuals, learning aids, and lesson plans. I also gained experince in building and maintaining a variety of circuitry, including a set of ANET A8 3D printers.

Currently I am looking to apply my teaching credential and looking for work in California, ideally near the Bay Area.

I also do a lot of reading and writing. I have published a science fiction novel through Amazon (sequel in the works). And I have a blog where I post my thoughts and recommendations about the books I read; though I have not had time to update the blog as of late.
